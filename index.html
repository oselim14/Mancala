mancala

How to play:

1. The game begins with player 1 selecting a pocket on their side. These pockets are highlighted during your turn.
2. The game moves in a counter-clockwise rotation and deposits 1 stone in each pocket along the way until the stones run out.
3. The game will skip the other players mancala, only placing your stones in the pockets and your mancala.
4. If the final stone is placed in your mancala, your turn continues and you may select another pocket.
5. The game ends when either player has collected more than 24 stones.
6. Press the "Play Again!" button if you'd like to try again.
